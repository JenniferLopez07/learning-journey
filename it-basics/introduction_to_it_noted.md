# Introduction to IT – Codecademy 

## Overview
This course covers the fundamentals of Information Technology, including hardware, software, networking, and system administration.  
It provides the foundation needed before diving deeper into cybersecurity.

## Key Concepts
- Hardware vs. software
- Operating systems (Windows, Linux, macOS)
- Networking basics (IP addresses, DNS, TCP/IP)
- Security principles (firewalls, VPNs)
- IT support and troubleshooting

## Examples
- Diagram of the OSI model
- Common networking commands: `ping`, `tracert`, `ipconfig`

## Takeaways
- IT fundamentals are the backbone of cybersecurity — understanding systems helps identify vulnerabilities.  
- Networking knowledge connects directly to penetration testing and forensic analysis.
